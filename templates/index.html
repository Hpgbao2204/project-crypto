Attribute-Based Encryption

Encrypt your documents with fine-grained access policies. Only users with matching attributes can decrypt and access your data.

Digital Signatures

Sign your documents digitally to ensure authenticity and integrity. Verify signatures to confirm the document hasn't been tampered with.

Secure Sharing

Share your documents securely with specific individuals or groups based on their attributes and roles.

How It Works
  1. Upload your document to the system
  2. Choose an encryption method (MA-ABE or Hybrid ABE)
  3. Define an access policy using attributes (e.g., "Doctor@Hospital OR Admin@Hospital")
  4. Encrypt and store your document securely
  5. Optionally sign your document with your digital signature
  6. Share the encrypted document with others

Only users whose attributes satisfy the access policy will be able to decrypt and view the document.

Security Features
  • Attribute-Based Access Control: Fine-grained control over who can access your documents
  • End-to-End Encryption: Documents are encrypted before leaving your browser
  • Digital Signatures: Verify document authenticity and integrity
  • Secure Key Management: Private keys are encrypted with your password
  • Audit Trail: Track document access and modifications
